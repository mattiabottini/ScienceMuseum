How to arrive

By Train

  1. Arrive at Union Station of Denver (CO)

  2. Take the free couch called "STATION-MUSEUM BUS "

  3. Walk for 300 ft to reach the entrance of the museum

By Plane

  1. Arrive at Denver International Airport (CO)

  2. Take the free couch called "AIRPORT-MUSEUM BUS "

  3. Walk for 100 ft to reach the entrance of the museum

By Car

  • From North: travel along the route #62 and then exit #43

  • From South: travel along the highway #75 and then exit #34

  • From East: travel along the motorway #32 and then exit #33

  • From West: travel along the freeway #22 and then exit #5
